The orientation and my first two weeks have shown me how different "working" is from "studying":

The Team Process: I’ve learned that coding is only one part of the job. Working in an Agile environment means communicating daily and making sure my work fits into the bigger team goal.
Professional Mindset: I am learning to move from "learning for a test" to "learning to build real solutions" that people will actually use.
